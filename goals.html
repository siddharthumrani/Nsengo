Sustainable Development Goals

We help to build an ecosystem around the smallholder farmers

NSENGO believes that technology is the key to transforming Africa’s agriculture. When Africans have greater access to technology, they have digital access to financial aid. This enables farmers to purchase seeds, fertilizer, insurance, equipment, and any other tools they need for their farms to succeed.

Better technology also gives these farmers improved informational resources. They can find accurate weather forecasts and adjust their planting and harvesting accordingly. In addition, they can learn about the latest farming techniques which will help improve both their crop yields and productivity.

Funding Africa’s farmers is an ambitious endeavor. But we believe that, with microfinance, it will be possible. With the help of digital technology and broadband internet, reaching farmers in rural areas (primarily via branchless banking solutions) becomes less costly. Financial providers will no longer be reluctant to help smallholder farmers.

This is by no means an exhaustive list of challenges for African farmers. However, NSENGO believes that proper financial access and adequate job training can solve most of the challenges these farmers face. We want to see these smallholder farmers experience self-sufficiency and success. Our technological solutions enable smallholder farmers to effectively obtain the financial and technical support they need to sustain their livelihoods. When individuals succeed, the overall economy grows.
