About NSENGO

We help to build an ecosystem around the smallholder farmers

In the language of the Republic of Congo, “nsengo” means “hoe.” This multi-purpose farming tool is indispensable for farmers as they prepare their fields for planting and remove weeds. It’s the symbol of farming in Africa. Just as the hoe helps plants grow strong and healthy, NSENGO aims to help smallholder farmers grow their businesses.


Help & Support

Financial Security

Finance is an absolute necessity to solve these challenges. NSENGO wants to transform African agriculture. We believe we can make this possible by enabling digital financial inclusion for smallholder farmers by giving them access to affordable microfinance and a holistic technology platform.

Small Holder Farmer Focus

Africa has enormous untapped agricultural potential. Even though smallholder farmers make up 70% of the African population, 240 million Africans still go hungry. NSENGO is focused on smallholder farmers in our pursuit of the United Nations’ Sustainable Development Goals Agenda to end global hunger and extreme poverty.

Technology Enable

We believe technology is the key to give these smallholder farmers access to microfinance and to connect them with important players in the ecosystem. Profitable business for smallholder farmers is the best way to achieve global food security and eliminate poverty.


Vision

Our vision is to contribute to the green revolution in Africa by helping smallholder farmers feed their continent and live out of poverty.
