Investors

Do you want to make the world a better place? You can by providing the necessities that others lack. For the people of Africa, some of the major needs are agricultural. Africa is rich in culture and heritage, but some of the challenges the population faces prevent it from reaching its full potential. NSENGO wants to eliminate hunger and poverty while helping smallholder farmers.

Two hundred forty million people are going hungry right now on the African continent. The average income for a farmer household is just $6.32 per day. This situation must change. NSENGO wants to contribute to the growth of Africa. We want to rid smallholder farmers of the problems they are facing.

If you or your company wants to make a difference with their investments—to invest in more than just a good financial return—connect with us at invest@nsengo.com !

Partners

Many smallholder farmers in Africa have a desperate need for financial services. Unfortunately, most financial service providers consider smallholder farmers (and agriculture workers in general) a high-risk group due to their lack of financial knowledge, cash flow, and credit history. Providers are reluctant to offer services to farmers when there are high costs associated with establishing and maintaining branches in rural areas.

We are confident in our mission, but we need your help and support! We welcome you to join us on this journey! Connect with us by emailing partners@nsengo.com
